INTERESTS

Board Games

These are most of the board games that I own.


I am a board game fanatic. To me there is nothing better than game night, having a good time with friends snacking and playing for hours. I love the ability to shift into a roll and sort of leave reality for the moment, using my mind a and strategizing to either beat my competitors or, in cases of cooperative games, to beat the game. These images are most of the games I own, there are a handful of others not depicted. I mentioned this in my ice-breaker post, but in total I have about $1,500 worth of games, and I don't plan on stopping soon. My current favorites would have to be Blood Rage, Cyclades, and Shadows Over Camelot. Blood Rage is a game that takes place during ragnarok, where you play as norse tribes battling and plundering and dying for glory all while the world ends around you. Cydlades is a game where you play as an ancient Greek civilizations seeking the Gods blessing in your quest to expand to various islands building armories and ports in the hope of constructing twh metropolises. Shadows over camelot is a cooperative game where you and your friends play various knights of the round table, fighting off the enemies beseiging Camelot while you try and conquer quests, like the quest for the Holy Grail. But be careful, one of you may be a spy.
